Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Welcome to Clotheline. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclosure, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that allows us to provide our laundry pickup and delivery services efficiently:

  • Personal Data: Name, phone number, and email address for account creation and communication.
  • Location Data: Geolocation data to coordinate pickup and delivery drivers.
  • Payment Data: Payment details are processed securely by our third-party payment processors. We do not store your credit card information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Facilitate laundry pickup and delivery.
  • Process payments and refunds.
  • Send you order updates and service notifications.
  • Improve our app functionality and user experience.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Laundromat partners and delivery drivers solely for the purpose of fulfilling your order.
  • Legal Obligations: If required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@brimarcglobal.com.
