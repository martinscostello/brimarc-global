Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

At Brimarc Global, and through our application Livaro, we prioritize your privacy above all else. This Privacy Policy outlines how your data is handled.

1. Data Storage

Livaro is a local-first application. This means:

  • All your documents, IDs, reminders, and personal information are stored directly on your device.
  • We do not upload your data to any cloud server, database, or third-party storage.
  • We cannot access, view, or recover your data if your device is lost, because we do not have it.

2. No Account Required

You do not need to create an account to use Livaro. We do not collect your email address, phone number, or social profiles.

3. Usage of Device Features

Livaro may request access to certain device features to function correctly:

  • Camera/Gallery: To scan documents or add images to your vault. This data remains local.
  • Biometrics (Face ID / Fingerprint): To unlock the app securely. We rely on the device's native security system and do not store your biometric data ourselves.
  • Notifications: To send you local reminders for expiries and deadlines.

4. Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregate usage data (e.g., "app crash reports" or "feature usage counts") to improve app stability. This data is fully anonymized and cannot be linked back to you or your specific documents.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@brimarcglobal.com.
