Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Devote respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

🔹 Personal Information

Name, Email, and Account details.

🔹 Usage Data

App interactions, features used, and session data.

🔹 Device Information & Hardware Permissions

To provide full functionality, the App requires access to certain device features:

📸 Camera Access (android.permission.CAMERA): Required for scanning QR codes to join Sanctuary rooms and for taking profile identification photos. The camera is only active when you trigger these specific features.

🎤 Microphone Access (android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO): Used for interactive spiritual features and audio-based devotions.

🔔 Notifications: Used for prayer reminders, community updates, and teaching alerts.

🔹 Optional Data

Prayer activity and Journal entries (if used).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use data to:

  • Provide and improve the App's spiritual features.
  • Personalize content and maintain high-fidelity security.
  • Send notifications (if enabled).

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers with industry-standard encryption and access controls. While we implement rigorous safeguards to protect your data, no system is 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. We may share data only with:

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics platforms like Firebase).
  • Legal authorities (only if strictly required by law).

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may:

  • Access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. You can submit a deletion request via our Account Deletion Page.
  • Withdraw consent for hardware permissions (Camera/Microphone) at any time via your device settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

Devote is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements.

8. Contact Us

For questions or data requests, please contact us at:
