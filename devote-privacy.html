Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Devote respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Overview

Devote respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

🔹 Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email
  • Account details

🔹 Usage Data

  • App interactions
  • Features used
  • Session data

🔹 Device Information

  • Device type
  • OS version
  • Unique identifiers

🔹 Optional Data

  • Prayer activity
  • Journal entries (if used)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use data to:

  • Provide and improve the App
  • Personalize spiritual content
  • Send notifications (if enabled)
  • Maintain security

4. Notifications

We may send:

  • Prayer reminders
  • Community updates
  • Teaching alerts

You can disable notifications anytime.

5. Data Storage

Your data may be stored on:

  • Secure cloud servers
  • Third-party infrastructure

We implement safeguards to protect your data.

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data.

We may share data with:

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics)
  • Payment processors
  • Legal authorities (if required)

7. Third-Party Services

We may use:

  • Firebase (analytics, messaging)
  • Payment providers (future use)

These services have their own privacy policies.

8. Data Retention

We retain data:

  • As long as your account is active
  • As needed for legal obligations

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction
  • Request deletion
  • Withdraw consent

10. Children’s Privacy

Devote is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Security

We implement:

  • Encryption
  • Secure authentication
  • Access controls

However, no system is 100% secure.

12. International Users

Your data may be processed outside your country.

13. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy.

14. Contact

For questions: Support@brimarcglobal.com
