Daily Profit

Run Your Business With Clarity

DailyProfit is a cloud-based business tracking app designed for small business owners who want to understand their numbers without spreadsheets or accounting stress. Track sales, manage expenses, see real profit, and get clear tax insights — all in one simple app.

Track Sales & Expenses Effortlessly

Record daily sales and business expenses in seconds. DailyProfit automatically calculates your profit so you always know how your business is performing.

✓ Add sales and expenses anytime
✓ Categorize products and costs
✓ View daily, monthly, and custom reports

No accounting knowledge required.

Know Your Real Profit

DailyProfit shows you exactly what you make after expenses — not just how much you sell. Real-time profit calculation, clear breakdown of sales vs expenses, and easy-to-read summaries.

No guessing. No confusion.

Smart Tax Insight

(Educational Only)

Understand your possible tax position without fear or jargon. DailyProfit provides tax estimates for guidance, based on your business type, profit, sales, and tax-exempt items.

You’ll see what is taxable, what is exempt, and why a tax applies or doesn’t. This feature helps you prepare ahead, not panic.

DailyProfit does not file taxes or replace professional advice.

Work With Your Team — Safely

Invite managers, sales staff, or auditors and assign controlled access to each role. Perfect for growing businesses.

✓ Invite team members via QR code
✓ Assign permissions per role
✓ Control access to sales/expenses
✓ Monitor activity for accountability

Cloud Sync & Multi-Device Access

Your data is securely stored in the cloud and synced across devices. Access your business from multiple phones and keep data up to date in real time. Ideal for owners and teams working together.

Your data stays yours.

Reports & Exports

Generate monthly summaries, custom date ranges, and export to PDF or CSV to share with your accountant.
